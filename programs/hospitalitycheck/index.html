HospitalityCheck®

A targeted cleaning and maintenance program designed for the Hospitality Industry.

90% of travelers avoid hotels labeled as dirty.

Quality training, effective management and the right products are essential for maintaining a clean and healthy facility. HospitalityCheck® addresses your unique needs by integrating knowledge, resources, and products into a dedicated program that delivers powerful results.

Recognizing Your Critical Business Needs

Labor, guests and costs all represent critical business needs that must be addressed to sustain and grow your business.

Labor Readiness

With nearly half of the expenses related to labor, and a high turnover rate, an inability to attract, hire and retain an efficient workforce can seriously impact your facility’s reputation, morale and profitability.

Guest Satisfaction

Negative guest experiences have a direct correlation with vacancy rates. Word-of-mouth opinions, coupled with far-reaching online reviews, truly put your destiny in the hands of your guests.

Cost Containment

To stay competitive you must meticulously manage the bottom line. Having the right people, processes, and products in place minimizes costs and maximizes your margins across the operation.

CleanCheck® Training System

CleanCheck® is a thorough, cost-effective program that trains employees on keeping a facility clean and compliant. CleanCheck® modules cover basic and advanced topics for cleaning a variety of areas including restrooms, hard floors, carpet, etc. Safety modules ensure OSHA, Bloodborne Pathogen Standard, and GHS compliance.

  • Web - or DVD-based video tutorials
  • Web - or paper-based testing
  • Training manuals
  • Compliance documentation and tracking
  • On-the-job cards
  • Employee recognition certificates

CompuClean®

Spartan Chemical’s cloud-based CompuClean Facility Management Software provides an integrated reporting system and a variety of modules to improve the workloading, quality assurance, inventory management, and documentation of your facility’s cleaning operations, enabling you to:

  • Identify training/employee issues 
  • Improve health and safety for customers
  • Improve facility cleanliness
  • Minimize operational costs
  • Evaluate processes and workloading 
  • Satisfy customers

Now, with the new CompuClean Mobile App, it has never been easier to track and manage cleaning operations whenever and wherever you are.
