Building Service Contractor Program

“Your priorities are our top priority.”

As a Building Service Contractor, you put your reputation on the line every day.  Providing first-class service and results for your clients is a daily target.

To stay competitive, you need:

  • A well-trained staff
  • The right products
  • Efficient management tools

Highly focused on supporting Building Service Contractors, Spartan offers strategic solutions that will help you gain efficiencies while streamlining your business growth. For more than 60 years, Spartan has been delivering national brand product excellence backed by the exceptional services you need to meet and exceed industry standards and stay up to date with best practices.

Committed to Building Service Contractors

Providing Expertise, Products, and Programs to Grow Your Business

CompuClean® for Building Service Contractors

Spartan Chemical’s CompuClean® Cleaning Management System provides a simple, cloud-based set of tools to help any Building Service Contractor more effectively manage operations. Contractors can expect to reduce paperwork, improve mobility, gain insight into operational performance, and improve work scheduling and customer feedback processes by utilizing CompuClean as part of the management process.

CleanCheck® Training System

CleanCheck® is a thorough, cost-effective program that trains employees on keeping a facility clean and compliant. CleanCheck® modules cover basic and advanced topics for cleaning a variety of areas including restrooms, hard floors, carpet, etc. Safety modules ensure OSHA, Bloodborne Pathogen Standard, and GHS compliance.

  • Web - or DVD-based video tutorials
  • Web - or paper-based testing
  • Training manuals
  • Compliance documentation and tracking
  • On-the-job cards
  • Employee recognition certificates

CompuClean®

Spartan Chemical’s cloud-based CompuClean Facility Management Software provides an integrated reporting system and a variety of modules to improve the workloading, quality assurance, inventory management, and documentation of your facility’s cleaning operations, enabling you to:

  • Identify training/employee issues 
  • Improve health and safety for customers
  • Improve facility cleanliness
  • Minimize operational costs
  • Evaluate processes and workloading 
  • Satisfy customers

Now, with the new CompuClean Mobile App, it has never been easier to track and manage cleaning operations whenever and wherever you are.
