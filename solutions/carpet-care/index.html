A Complete System for Maintaining Carpeted Flooring

Spartan’s carpet care products provide the highest quality solutions with easy-to-follow procedures, ensuring  optimum carpet appearance and rejuvenation at minimal cost. From traditional carpet cleaning to green cleaning methods, give your carpet a high quality touch with Spartan’s carpet care maintenance program – guaranteed to deep clean and beautify.

Featured Products

Spartan’s complete line of carpet care products, including the Contempo® line, can be used for a wide variety of carpet care solutions such as: specialty spot removers, total carpet stain protection, general purpose, deep cleaning, and malodor counteractants. Plus, it includes the latest in bio-augmentation and a first of its kind, Spartan’s extraction carpet cleaner, CX3 Bio-assist®.

Extraction Cleaning

Spartan offers a variety of extraction products including environmentally preferable options.

  • Contempo V®

  • Xtraction II®

  • Clean by Peroxy®

  • Green Solutions® Carpet Cleaner

Odor Elimination

Spartan’s odor eliminating carpet care products attack malodors at the source, leaving the room smelling fresh and clean.

  • CX3 Bio-Assist®

  • Consume®

  • Contempo® Carpet Refresher

Stain Removal

Spartan offers solutions for all of your stain removal needs, including a spotter kit, made up of a variety of formulas to address stains from coffee, to lipstick to organic soils.

  • SSE Carpet Prespray & Spotter®

  • Contempo® H2O2 Spotting Solution

  • Carpet Stain Remover

  • Contempo® Deluxe Spotting Kit

Specialty Care Solutions

It happens every year – gum embedded in the carpet. Spartan can take care of that for you and a whole host of other specialty cases including bonnet cleaning, yellowing, and protection.

  • Contempo® Tannin Browning Treatment

  • Defoamer

  • Spartagard®

  • Chewing Gum Remover
